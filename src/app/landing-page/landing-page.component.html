Welcome to Open Enzyme Database!

A community-driven resource for researchers and professionals in biochemistry, molecular biology, enzymology, protein and metabolic engineering, and related fields, the Open Enzyme Database offers curated data on enzyme kinetics and experimental parameters, ongoing algorithm development, an expanding knowledge base of enzyme solubility, stability, specificity, structures, functions and associated enzymatic reactions.
Learn more about how the Open Enzyme Database works below and get started using the tool today.
